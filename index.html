Our story

Boho Byte Bags started in a quiet corner of my rural farmhouse. At the time, I was working from home as a technical writer for a big company, but what really bothered me were the backpacks we all carried to our twice-yearly company meetings.

They were "one-size-fits-all," drab and lifeless, with standard pockets that didn't fit anything. None of them worked really well, and none of them were very stylish. I knew this from experience, because over the years I had collected a 10x10 storage locker full of them!

One day, while trying to pack my bag for another overseas trip, I finally had enough. A sewist for many years, I set up my machine, grabbed the only colorful material I had around -- some boho scraps -- and started building a custom bag that looked great and fit my stuff like a glove.

When I got to the meeting, my team couldn't stop talking about my bag, how great it looked, and how everything had a pocket that waa just right. Oh there were still flaws, but that didn't stop everyone I met from asking where I got the bag.

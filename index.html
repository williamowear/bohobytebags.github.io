Our story

Boho Byte Bags started in a quiet corner of my farmhouse bedroom. At the time, I was working from home as a technical writer for a big company, but what really bothered me were the backpacks we all carried to our twice-yearly company meetings.

They were "one-size-fits-all," drab and lifeless, with standard pockets that didn't fit anything well. None of them worked really well, and none of them were very stylish. I knew this from experience, because over the years I had collected a 10x10 storage locker full of them.
